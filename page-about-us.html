  • Our Journey

    Established in 1977, Trimline embarked on a mission to redefine the standards of printing and packaging. Over the years, we have evolved and diversified, adapting to the dynamic needs of the market.

  • International Expansion

    In 2004, Trimline expanded its horizons beyond national borders, venturing into international operations. This marked a significant turning point as we transitioned into a comprehensive solution provider, specializing in industrial raw materials and packaging.

  • Our Global Presence

    With a robust supply base in China, Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and the Middle East, Trimline has been a stalwart in the industrial raw materials and packaging industry since its inception in 1977. Over the years, we've strategically expanded our reach into PET, PS, PE (resin, film, sheet, and preforms), petrochemicals, textiles, and fashion wear.

  • Diversification and Specialization

    Today, Trimline stands as a comprehensive solution provider, specializing not only in raw materials and packaging but also in finished goods, including textiles and fashion wear. Our expertise extends to sourcing and marketing a diverse range of textile products such as staple fiber, polyester yarn, nylon yarn, viscose yarn, cloth, and various polymer-related items.

  • Project Commitments and Operating Philosophy

    Trimline is recognized for its project commitments, operational speed, quality practices, and an overall customer-centric approach. Handling business worth millions of dollars, our operating philosophy is centered around providing buyers with products that meet their specifications, delivered reliably and at a reasonable price in both domestic and international markets.

  • Global Opportunities, Local Values

    Trimline provides its customers with a global network of opportunities while maintaining a commitment to the best deals possible. Our journey is a testament to our dedication to excellence, transparency, and reliability.

Our Vision

At Trimline, we envision a future where innovation and sustainability converge seamlessly. We are not just a company; we are a dynamic force driving positive change in the industry.

Mission and Expertise

Our mission is simple yet profound: to offer the most competitive prices to our valued clientele. We've successfully forged strong relationships with manufacturers, converters, petrochemical complexes, and traders in major supply and manufacturing hubs like China, Taiwan, Korea, Thailand, and the Middle East. This expertise has earned us the reputation of being a Global Supply Chain Consultant.

OTHER VENTURES

Our commitment to innovation and growth has led us to explore and invest in various ventures. These may include Trimline Commodities, Trade Financers, ITCG, Fadano & Khazanay, where we apply the same principles of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction that define Trimline.
