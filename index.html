About Us

Established in 1977, Trimline embarked on a mission to redefine the standards of printing and packaging. Over the years, we have evolved and diversified, adapting to the dynamic needs of the market.

Today, Trimline proudly serves as an Internal Supply Chain Consultant and Marketing Company. With a wealth of experience and a commitment to excellence, we navigate the intricate landscapes of industrial raw materials and packaging, providing tailored solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients.
