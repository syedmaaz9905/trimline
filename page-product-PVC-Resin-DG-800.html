Grade DG-800 is ethylene based Polyvinyl Chloride Resin with polymerization degree around 800, it corresponds to GB/T 5761-2018 SG -7. Its typical properties are with good barrier and transparency, the "fish eye" index fulfills high requirements, it's our major product. Mainly used in transparent sheet, medical sheet and containers.

Packaging is Optional in 25kg, 600kg bag or 1200KG Jumbo bag.
